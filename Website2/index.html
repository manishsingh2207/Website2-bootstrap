Sunburn 2015

The 9th edition is another 4 day event at Vagator, Goa. On November 20, 2015 Sunburn Goa's international ticketing partner, viagogo, announced that the festival has become one of the largest international music festivals in Asia, with people from more than 42 countries buying tickets on the viagogo platform. On November 24, 2015, the management of Sunburn Goa also released a statement citing this fact and proving to fans in India that they did not need to travel half way around the world to get the same experience as Festival Tomorrowland. Sunburn Goa 2015 is expected to draw the largest crowds since its inception.

ULTRA MIAMI 2015

Ultra Music Festival (UMF) is an annual outdoor electronic music festival that occurs in March in the city of Miami, Florida, United States. The festival, which was founded in 1999 by Russell Faibisch and Alex Omes, is named after the 1997 Depeche Mode album, Ultra. Ultra Music Festival coincides with the annual Winter Music Conference, which is also held in Miami.
